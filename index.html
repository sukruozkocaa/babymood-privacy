Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

1. Introduction

BabyMood ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Our app provides AI-powered analysis of baby sounds, videos, and images, along with sleep sound features to help parents better understand and care for their babies.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide:

  • Audio recordings of baby sounds (cries, coos, etc.) for analysis
  • Video recordings of baby activities for analysis
  • Photos/images of baby for analysis
  • App preferences and settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (model, OS version, unique identifiers)
  • App usage data and analytics
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • IP address and general location data

2.3 Third-Party Services:

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase Analytics - App usage and performance
  • Google AdMob - Advertising (for free users)
  • RevenueCat - Subscription management

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered analysis of baby sounds, videos, and images
  • Process audio, video, and image recordings to provide insights about your baby's needs
  • Deliver sleep sound content and features
  • Personalize your app experience
  • Process premium purchases and manage subscriptions
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Enhance our AI analysis models using aggregated, anonymized data
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Audio, video, and image recordings are processed securely and are not stored permanently unless you choose to save them within the app
  • Sleep sounds are streamed or cached locally on your device and do not require data transmission
  • We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data during transmission and storage
  • Data is processed on secure servers with restricted access
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access
  • No user account or login credentials are stored as the app does not require authentication

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (cloud hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Third parties with your explicit consent

Our AI analysis may use aggregated, anonymized data from audio, video, and image recordings to improve our models, but this data cannot be traced back to individual users or specific babies.

6. Media Processing and Analysis

6.1 Audio Analysis

When you use our audio analysis feature, recordings are processed using AI to identify patterns such as crying, cooing, or other baby sounds. Analysis occurs either on your device or on our secure servers, depending on the feature used.

6.2 Video Analysis

Video recordings are analyzed to detect baby movements, facial expressions, and activities. Processing is performed securely and videos are not permanently stored unless you explicitly save them.

6.3 Image Analysis

Photos or images you submit are analyzed for relevant baby-related insights. Images are processed securely and are not retained beyond the analysis session unless you choose to save them.

6.4 Sleep Sounds

Our sleep sound library is provided for your convenience. These sounds are either streamed or cached locally on your device and do not involve transmission of personal data.

You have the right to:

  • Delete any saved recordings from within the app
  • Clear app data through your device settings
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking (where technically feasible)
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
